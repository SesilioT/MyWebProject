About Me

Born on April 25, 1982, in the Bronx, New York, I am the oldest of seven children, raised by two hardworking immigrant parents from the Dominican Republic. My journey has taken me from the vibrant streets of New York to a new chapter in Switzerland, where I moved in 2018 with my girlfriend, now wife.

Above all, I am a father, and my beautiful daughter means the world to me. I strive every day to be better, not just for myself but for her and to help others along the way. I believe in living life to the fullest, taking risks, and always getting back up after every fall.

My daughter, music, dance, and laughter fuel my soul, and no matter what life throws my way, I keep rolling with the punches and moving forward with a smile. I believe that failure is just a step closer towards success, because as Denzel Washington once said: "If you don’t fail, you’re not even trying."

So take risks, fail, and learn from your mistakes to achieve your goals and live a life without regrets.
